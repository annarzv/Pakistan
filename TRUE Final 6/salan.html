Karahi Chicken.

A beloved dish known for its rich, aromatic spices and succulent chicken simmered in a spicy tomato-based gravy. Cooked in a traditional "karahi" (wok), this dish features a flavorful blend of cumin, coriander, and garam masala. Garnished with fresh cilantro and ginger, it's best enjoyed with naan or rice, capturing the vibrant essence of Pakistani cuisine.

📝 Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 lb chicken thigh
  • 2 tbsp red chili pepper
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp black pepper

📝 Recipe

  1. Sautee diced onions onto pan with oil
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and stir
  3. Add all your spices & stir
  4. Add chicken & stir until cooked
  5. Add tomatoes & stir. Let sit until tomatoes are softened completely
  6. Adjust spices as needed
  7. Slow cook on low for ~40 minutes
  8. Enjoy with rice or roti ㋡
