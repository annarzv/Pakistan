Mango Lassi.

Mango Lassi is a popular and refreshing Pakistani drink that combines the sweetness of ripe mangoes with the creamy richness of yogurt. This delicious beverage, often enjoyed during the hot summer months, is perfect for cooling down and indulging in a taste of tropical paradise.

üìù Ingredients

  • 1 cup ripe mango pulp (fresh or canned)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2-3 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Sliced mango or a few mint leaves (for garnish)

üìù Recipe

  1. If using fresh mangoes, peel and chop the mangoes, then blend to make a smooth pulp.
  2. If using canned mango pulp, measure out 1 cup.
  3. In a blender, combine the mango pulp, yogurt, milk, and sugar.
  4. Add the ground cardamom if using.
  5. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  6. If the lassi is too thick, add a little more milk and blend again until you reach the desired consistency.
